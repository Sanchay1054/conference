How to get Perundurai
Kongu Engineering College is well connected by road transport. The nearest city is Erode, which has a railway junction and bus terminus, but no airport.
By Air
There are two main air routes to reach the conference venue:
1. Coimbatore International Airport: Located 80 km from Perundurai. From there, take a bus to reach Perundurai.
2. Chennai International Airport: Located 410 km from Perundurai. From there, take a bus or train to Erode, and then board a bus to reach Perundurai.
By Train
Most northern states of India are well connected to Coimbatore by railways.
From Coimbatore, board a bus to reach Perundurai.
