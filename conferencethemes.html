Author Guidelines:
  • Plagiarism must be less than 10% (excluding references). Plagiarism will be checked for conference presentation and publication.
  • The use of AI-generated writing, including ChatGPT is strictly prohibited.
  • There must be no grammatical errors. High language clarity is expected, and a language expert should review the final manuscript. Any disputes may lead to direct rejection by the journal.
  • Figure or table should NOT be copied from any other paper or the internet. This activity may lead to direct rejection. Ensure that the figures are of high resolution.
  • Page limit: Minimum six pages and maximum eight pages, adhering to the journal template.
  • Each paper must include an abstract (150-200 words) and 4-5 keywords.
  • All reference numbers/citations must be properly cited within the manuscript.
  • Ensure that all references are cited in the body of the paper.
  • Ensure that all figures and tables are numbered in proper order
  • NO table should be in image format.
  • All mathematical equations must be prepared using a math editor; no equation should be in image format.
  • Maintain high-quality (HD) figures.
Call for paper
The authors are kindly invited to submit their original, unpublished research articles that are not under review by any other conference or journal. Only accepted and registered articles will be permitted for presentation at the conference. At least one author of each accepted article in the book proceedings must register for the conference. Papers from unregistered authors will not be included in the book proceedings.

Publications
The following journals have confirmed the proposal of the special issues of ICAAC 2025 to publish the extended version of the selected papers presented during conference (through invited submissions only).
(1) All high quality papers will be published in the Taylor & Francis as a proceedings book entitled "Applied Mathematics, Automation, and Computing" with a minimum publication charge.
(2) Remaining selected papers will be published as the Scopus-indexed book chapters with additional charge.
Note: The publication of will high quality articles will be as per the journal guidelines and review processes. Authors will be informed to submit the extended version of the presented papers after the conference.
