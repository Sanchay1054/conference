About
Welcome to the International Conference on Applied Mathematics, Automation, and Computing (ICAMAC). This prestigious event serves as a global platform for researchers, academics, and industry professionals to share their innovative ideas and breakthroughs in the fields of applied mathematics, automation, and computing. ICAMAC aims to foster collaboration, spark insightful discussions, and advance knowledge across these dynamic disciplines. Join us for a series of inspiring keynote speeches, technical sessions, and networking opportunities that will connect you with thought leaders and experts from around the world. ICAMAC 2025 promises an exhilarating program designed to expand the frontiers of applied mathematics and computer science. The sessions are crafted to inspire intellectual growth and promote interdisciplinary collaborations, which are vital for driving innovation across diverse scientific fields. Moreover, the conference provides an exceptional platform for young researchers to connect with top decision-makers and experts in mathematics and applied computing. This blend of diverse perspectives is crucial for tackling the real-world challenges these disciplines encounter today. Join us at ICAMAC 2025 as we shape the future of these vital fields. Experience an exhilarating journey in the breathtaking city of Erode, where tradition and innovation converge to create a stimulating environment for intellectual exchange. Mark your calendars and get ready to contribute to and benefit from this esteemed international event.
